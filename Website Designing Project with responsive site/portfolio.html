E-commerce Platform

Project Overview

We developed a comprehensive e-commerce platform for a retail client looking to expand their online presence. The platform includes product management, inventory tracking, payment processing, and customer analytics.

Technologies Used

  • Frontend: React.js, Bootstrap
  • Backend: Node.js, Express
  • Database: MongoDB
  • Payment Processing: Stripe API
  • Hosting: AWS

Key Features

  • Responsive design for all devices
  • Advanced search and filtering
  • Secure payment processing
  • Inventory management system
  • Customer account management
  • Order tracking and history
  • Analytics dashboard

Results

The platform resulted in a 45% increase in online sales within the first three months, with a 30% improvement in customer retention. The client also saw a significant reduction in operational costs due to the automated inventory management system.
