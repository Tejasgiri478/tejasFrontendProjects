Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical design project take?

The timeline varies depending on the scope and complexity of the project. A simple website design might take 2-4 weeks, while a comprehensive app design could take 2-3 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the initial consultation.

Do you offer maintenance services after the project is completed?

Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance and support services to ensure your website or application continues to perform optimally. We have different maintenance packages available to suit your needs.

How do you handle revisions and feedback?

We have a structured feedback process where clients can provide comments and suggestions at key milestones. The number of revision rounds depends on your package, but we're always committed to ensuring you're completely satisfied with the final product.

Do you work with clients internationally?

Absolutely! We work with clients from around the world. Our team is experienced in managing remote collaborations effectively, using various communication and project management tools to ensure smooth workflow regardless of time zones.

What information do you need to start a project?

To get started, we typically need information about your business, target audience, project goals, design preferences, and any existing brand guidelines. We'll provide a detailed questionnaire to help gather all the necessary information.

Do you provide hosting services?

While we don't provide hosting services directly, we can recommend reliable hosting providers based on your specific needs and help with the setup process. We ensure that your website is optimized for the hosting environment.
